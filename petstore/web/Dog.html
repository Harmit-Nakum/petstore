Shih Tzu

The Shih Tzu is an ancient breed that originated in Tibet, where it was highly regarded as a companion and lap dog. It is believed to be a cross between the Tibetan Lhasa Apso and various Chinese breeds, including the Pekingese.

Golden Retriever

The Golden Retriever is a breed that originated in Scotland in the mid-19th century. It was developed by Lord Tweedmouth, who crossed a yellow retriever with a Tweed Water Spaniel, resulting in the foundation of the breed.
